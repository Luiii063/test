About UNLAD Dolores

Distribution of Educational Assistance

e-Submit Scholarship Application

UNLAD Dolores educational assistance program was initiated in 2014 and has been dedicated to supporting the educational needs of college students ever since. As of 2019, the program continues to provide valuable assistance to students. The program has successfully helped a total of 500 scholars in achieving their educational goals. Our web-based system is designed to streamline the application process, allowing students to easily submit their required documents and track the progress of their applications online.
